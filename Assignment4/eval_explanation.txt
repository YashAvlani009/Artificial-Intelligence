Eval function explanation:

The eval function tries to find the optimal path using minmax algorithm so as to make player 1(max) win the game.
It calculates scores as the (score of Player 1- Score of player 2) corresponding to player 1.

Score:it is any possible consective 4 moves of same player on game board either horizontally, vertically or along the two main diagonals.
